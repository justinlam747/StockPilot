What the agent does
Six capabilities,
one agent
01
Low-Stock Detection
Scans every variant against your thresholds. Fires alerts the moment stock enters the danger zone.
02
Auto-Draft POs
Calculates optimal quantities from sell-through velocity and lead times. Drafts purchase orders for your approval.
03
Demand Forecasting
Analyzes 14-day trends to predict which SKUs will run out and when. Stays ahead of seasonal spikes.
04
Supplier Intelligence
Tracks lead times, reliability scores, and order history. Recommends the right supplier for each reorder.
05
Weekly Briefings
Generates inventory reports with top sellers, stockout events, and reorder recommendations. Timezone-aware scheduling.
06
Shopify Native
Embedded in your Shopify admin. Real-time sync via webhooks. Read-only access — we never modify your store.
