How It Works
Up and running in
five minutes
From install to your first AI-generated purchase order — no configuration headaches.
1
Install from Shopify
One click from the Shopify App Store. Authorize read-only access to your products, inventory, and orders — nothing else.
2
Your products sync instantly
StockPilot pulls every product, variant, and stock level from your store. The initial sync takes seconds, not hours.
3
Add your suppliers
Link suppliers to your products with lead times and contact info. StockPilot uses this to calculate optimal reorder timing.
4
Set your alert thresholds
Choose when you want to be notified — per product or globally. Set critical and warning levels that match your sell-through velocity.
5
AI takes over
The inventory agent monitors 24/7, fires alerts when stock drops, and auto-drafts purchase orders for your review. You approve, it sends.
Features

Real-Time Alerts
Configurable thresholds per variant. Get notified the moment stock drops below your comfort zone — not after it's already gone.
AI Purchase Orders
Claude AI analyzes your stock levels, sell-through rates, and supplier lead times to draft purchase orders automatically.

Automated inventory reports with trend analysis, top sellers, and stockout events. Delivered on your schedule, in your timezone.
Supplier Hub
Track lead times, contacts, and order history for every supplier. Link products to suppliers for smarter reorder recommendations.
Inventory Trends
14-day stock history for every variant. Spot patterns, predict shortages, and make data-driven purchasing decisions.
GDPR Compliant
Full data export, redaction, and deletion. We handle Shopify's mandatory webhooks so you stay compliant without lifting a finger.
